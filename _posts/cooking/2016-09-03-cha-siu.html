Ingredients

  • 3 pounds pork shoulder
  • 2 tablespoons white granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon five spice powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Chinese plum wine
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons hoisin sauce
  • 2 teaspoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons molasses
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 5 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon hot water

Directions

Marinade
  1. Cut pork into long strips (about 3" thick) and place into a gallon size ziplock bag
  2. In a medium size glass bowl, whisk together remaining ingredients
  3. Pour the marinade mixture into the bag, making sure to coat all the pork
  4. Allow to marinade overnight
Roasting
  1. Preheat the oven on bake to at least 475° F
  2. Line a roasting pan with aluminum foil and place a roasting rack on top
  3. Arrange pork on the rack, leaving 1-2" spaces between them
  4. Let the pork roast for 20 minutes on the middle rack
  5. Turn the pork and roast for another 15 minutes
  6. Baste the pork with a 1:1 mixture of honey and hoisin sauce, and roast for another 5 minutes
  7. Turn the pork again, baste, and roast for another 5 minutes
