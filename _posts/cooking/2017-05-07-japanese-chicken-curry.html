Ingredients

  • 3 small onions
  • 1 pound carrots
  • 6 Yukon gold potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 teaspoons ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 pounds chicken thighs
  • Kosher salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 3 cups chicken stock
  • 1 package Japanese curry roux (8.4 oz)
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Directions

Vegetable Preparation
  1. Cut each onion into 10 wedges
  2. Cut carrots into bite size pieces at a diagonal, rotating a quarter turn for each cut
  3. Cut potatoes into quarters and soak in water
  4. Mincs garlic
  5. Grate ginger

Meat Preparation

  1. Cut chicken into bite size pieces
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper

Cooking

  1. Press the "Saute" button and heat oil
  2. When the oil is hot, add onions, ginger, and garlic
  3. Add chicken and coat with oil
  4. Add carrots and potatoes and mix well
  5. Add chicken stock
  6. Add curry roux. Keep roux blocks on top.
  7. Press "Meat/Stew" button and set for 15 minutes
  8. When done, let the pressure release naturally
  9. Add ketchup and soy sauce and mix well
