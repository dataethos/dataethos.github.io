Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen mango
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup (2 oz) plain yogurt

Directions

  1. In a blender, add all ingredients and blend until smooth
  2. Transfer the mixture to an airtight freezer-safe container
  3. Freeze overnight
