Ingredients

  • 3 cups sweetened condensed milk (24 oz)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon lime zest
  • 3/4 cup lime juice

Directions

  1. Make the graham cracker crust
  2. Preheat the oven to 350° F
  3. In a medium bowl, combine all the ingredients.
  4. Mix together until smooth.
  5. Pour the mixture into the graham cracker crust.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 6 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely.
