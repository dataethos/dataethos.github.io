Ingredients

  • 250 grams mascarpone cheese
  • 400 grams lady finger biscuits
  • 250 grams heavy whipping cream
  • 7 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 cups strong coffee

Directions

  1. Whip cream and sugar until sugar dissoves
  2. Add cheese and blend until smooth
  3. Dip lady fingers in cooled coffee
  4. Layer lady fingers, cream mixture, and cocoa powder
