Ingredients

  • 3 pounds pork shoulder steak (1/2" thick)
  • 6 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons garlic
  • 3 tablespoons shallots
  • 3 stalks lemongrass (chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 1/2 teaspoons dark (black) soy sauce
  • 4 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Directions

Marinade
  1. Place the pork chops in a large container
  2. Add the remaining ingredients to a food processor and process to a paste
  3. Pour the marinade mixture over the pork, making sure to coat both sides
  4. Allow to marinade overnight
Searing
  1. Preheat a large griddle to medium-high
  2. Sear for 10 minutes on each side, until cooked through
  3. Remove from heat and allow to rest on a plate for 10 minutes, loosely covered with foil
