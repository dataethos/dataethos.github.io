Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham crachers crumbs (approximately 12 sheets)
  • 1/8 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1/8 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnaon
  • pinch of salt
  • 7 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325° F
  2. Finely grind graham crackers in a food processor.
  3. In a small bowl, combine all dry ingredients and mix.
  4. Add melted butter and mix with a fork.
  5. Press the mixture into a 9" pie pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes.
