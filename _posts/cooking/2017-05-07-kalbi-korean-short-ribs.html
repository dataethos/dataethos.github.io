Ingredients

  • 5 pounds beef short ribs
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 small pear
  • 4 tablespoons garlic
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

Meat Preparation

  1. Coat ribs with sugar
  2. Let sit for 10 minutes
Marinade
  1. Combine all other ingredients in a food processor
  2. Process until smooth
  3. Cover meat with mixture and marinade overnight
