Ingredients

  • 1 pound cream cheese (two 8 ounce packs)
  • 1/2 cup white granulated sugar
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 4 tablespoons sour cream
  • 4 tablespoons heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Make the graham cracker crust in the bottom of a 9" springform pan.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350° F.
  3. Using the paddle attachment, cream the cream cheese in a standing mixer.
  4. Add the sugar and continue mixing until combined.
  5. Add the egg yolks and continue mixing until smooth.
  6. Add sour cream, heavy whipping cream, and vanilla extract and mix.
  7. Whip egg whites to stiff peaks.
  8. Fold egg whites into the cream cheese mixture, a third at a time.
  9. Pour the mixture over the crust and bake for 45 minutes.
  10. Allow the cheesecake to cool overnight.
